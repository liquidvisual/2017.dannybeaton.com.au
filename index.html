An Exciting New Adventure Book for Children

"The night he watched his father die, Mal learned of the dark creatures who lurk in dreams, hungry for sleeping souls. Mal Winter, a young would-be hero, must traverse the dreamscape to learn the legacy of the Cloud Runners. He'll need to fight his greatest demons to save his family and friends from the unimaginable."

Danny is an Author & Illustrator from Sydney

Danny has written movies, short stories and even a poem or two, but the crowning jewel in his body of work is his latest book, Mal Winter and the Cloud Runners.

Danny values creativity and strives to bring this out in others. He is currently touring schools in Victoria, Australia with his creative workshop that promotes reading and creative writing.
